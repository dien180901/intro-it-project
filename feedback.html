feedback

#1. Dien Ma

Since our team has formed early in the sem-break, we have more time to prepare for our project. My team is the wonderful, which includes trustable and responsible people who always finish the workload before deadlines and helping the other team members If they have any blockers. This is the second semester of me, Thanh, and Tam so it’s is challenging for ours to meet the requirement for the good report in RMIT. However, my leader My is an excellent leader with the great management skills and she also help each member of team know how to write a report in RMIT with great citations. Thanh is a excellent time-management person, he also my teammate from the last semester, he also completes his workload before the deadline and very pleasant to help the other people. Tam is the creative person, he and My is the person who have contributed too much to our IT project with the excellent citations. My team also haves team meeting every week to assign task for each person and monitor the progress of this assignment. Hence, our project can finish before deadline without any pressure and stress on team members.

#2. Thanh Nguyen

Since we began working on our project quite early, we were able to quickly establish our first concepts. Tam and My were familiar to me from the previous semester, but I did not have the opportunity to collaborate with them. Dien, on the other hand, has been a friend of mine since the previous semester. I consider myself fortunate to have met my three fellow partners for this semester. They are enthusiastic, hardworking, and well-versed in their field. I could pick up a lot from them, from the way they solve difficulties to the ideas they have for projects. When compared to my initiatives, their projects are eye-opening experiences. Their ideas are practical, beneficial, and innovative in terms of our future well-being.

My is the most dedicated member of our group, in my opinion. She understands how to distribute assignments to each individual, and she completes all of her tasks on time. Tam and Dien, however, have quite different perspectives on technology; Tam’s is more insight-oriented, while Dien’s is more technical. They are both adept at dealing with the technological aspect. My, on the other hand, is skilled at writing and managing our collaborative process. I consider myself to be a supporting teammate who is there to assist them anytime they need it. Since our project ideas are similar to Tam’s and My’s first assignment project ideas, we decided to use them for our second and final projects. The majority of our projects include a variety of technology that I had never heard of before, requiring me to do extensive study to keep up with my teammate. I am glad of my team since we have not had any dispute while working on our project; otherwise, our procedure seems smooth sailing.

#3. My Nguyen

Our group has been forming since the early starting week of the semester. Although most of us have yet met each other, we are fairly on the same page as sharing the same target, vision, and expectation of the group work outcome. In overall, I found a great sense of friends from the group project even though I do not know these teammates beforehand. From my perspective, neither technical skills nor hard skills makes us a perform well in the assignment but rather our attitudes. To me, all of the three teammates are opened to share and give honest comments toward a discussion, they actively contribute ideas to improve the project which I am enjoyed working with. Every weekly meeting was delightful yet productive and outcome-driven that we met all the targeted discussed points. Besides than that, all the three teammates met weekly deadline sufficiently. Since I am not a student majoring IT, I am not excellent at technical as much as these friends. However, I am really lucky to have their support and instruction during the project. In other words, I learn from my teammates a lot which is useful for my development.

In regard to each person feedback, for Dien, creative and hard-working are the two traits I found the most in him which is believed to advocate him to achieve success in the future job. However, if I could give him an advice, it would be: “do not focus too heavy on your academic performance to forget other enjoyable parts of University’s life” because I used to be in such situation and now that I am about to graduate I find pretty regret of my time. For Thanh, he is little less engaged compared to the others but this is not a problem just because he is somewhat new to the university. Yet his performance in the group work proves that he is hard-working and competent for his career pathway. For Tam, with his previous experience in university life, he is doing his second degree at RMIT fairly well since he knows exactly his target for the journey. Tam actively contributes to group discussion which is good for the group improvement as well. In general, if I would have an overall advice to these friends as a person about to graduate, I would say: “do not waste your time on studying only, join more student activities so that you can explore more of your hidden abilities and talents”. If I had another chance to choose teammates for the course, I will definitely still go for these three responsible and hard-working teammates.

#4. Tam Kieu

My group formed in the early stage of the course. My teammates have worked together in previous courses, and I was the last teammate who joined this group. To be honest, there was some pressure for me due to their energy. But after quite a long working process, I know I was very lucky to be in this team. To me, they are talented in an unusual way.

Thanh is very good at time-management, he would always be the first one who finished the tasks, and at every meeting, he would never be late. He is industrious and never stops if he cannot understand the problems. For me, he needs to believe more in himself because he has an intense potential to reach the big things. For Dien, he is the one who opens my mind and guild me very much. He has a very big dream, and why I heard about that, I realized that I must set my bigger goal which will always be the motivation for working hard. Dien can work 12 hours a day to follow his plan. He’s also such a kind person, always takes the hardest task without any complaints. I sincerely hope he can reach his dream, he is born for it. For My, she is a good leader with excellent leadership and communication skills, her efforts to make a perfect report also surprises me very much. She always makes things perfect with her talent. I believe that not only me but also every person will feel very lucky to have a chance to work with her. If I have another chance to working with these three talented teammates in the future courses, I won’t have any hesitation in joining them.
